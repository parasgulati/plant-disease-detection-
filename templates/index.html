PlantDisPre

Just take a picture of the leaf of plant you want to examine and upload here

The application is developed using PyTorch Used Transfer Learning technique and developed the model on pretrained Densnet121 model Currently the application predicts the disease with upto 93% accuracy on following plants

the plants which can be examined:

  • Apple
  • Blueberry
  • Cherry_(including_sour)
  • Corn_(maize)
  • Grape
  • Orange
  • Peach
  • Pepper,_bell
  • Potato
  • Raspberry
  • Soybean
  • Squash
  • Strawberry
  • Tomato
